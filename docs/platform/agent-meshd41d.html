your AI Agents with the
Agent Mesh

Agentic AI systems are rapidly emerging as the new way to build applications. However, their autonomous operation makes them prone to unpredictable costs, results and behavior. fliplug's agent mesh brings sound governance to all your agents and LLMs.

Unified Agentic AI governance

Now AI Innovation does not have to be held back by security, governance and control concerns

the fliplug Agent Mesh brings discovery, analytics, control, insights and easy MCP tool generation.
Learn more

Unlock the full potential of AI with our LLM Gateway—a secure, scalable, and intelligent access layer between your applications and large language models. Designed for modern enterprises, our gateway provides fine-grained control over how AI is accessed and consumed across your organization. From traffic management and usage quotas to advanced routing and observability, the LLM Gateway ensures that your AI interactions are efficient, governed, and aligned with internal policies.

The Agent Catalog is your centralized hub for discovering, managing, and scaling autonomous agents across your organization. Much like a developer portal for APIs, it provides a unified interface where teams can explore available agents, understand their capabilities, and integrate them into workflows with ease. Whether you’re deploying customer service bots, internal copilots, or complex orchestration agents, the Agent Catalog makes discovery intuitive and actionable.

The Agent Tool Server acts as a powerful bridge between your existing APIs and your AI agents, allowing you to expose any API as an actionable tool through the Model Context Protocol (MCP). With just a few configurations, the Agent Tool Server transforms traditional services into AI-consumable interfaces—enabling agents to discover, invoke, and orchestrate real-world actions securely and intelligently. It’s the missing link that brings your APIs into the world of autonomous agents without rewriting a single line of backend code.

Multi Agent, Multi LLM

All your agents. All your LLMs.

Manage all your agents and LLMs and gain powerful insights into performance and cost optimization
Learn more

Whether you're integrating with OpenAI, Anthropic, or custom fine-tuned models hosted on private infrastructure, our gateway abstracts complexity while offering complete flexibility.

Add user-level authentication, enforce context-aware guardrails, and monitor usage trends in real time—all through a unified interface built for developers and platform teams. The result: faster, safer, and smarter AI adoption without compromising on control or compliance

An AI Gateway gives you enterprise-grade control over how large language models are accessed and used by acting as a secure, policy-driven layer between your applications and the LLM. It allows you to implement rate limiting to manage usage and prevent overages, inspect incoming and outgoing traffic to ensure sensitive data isn’t exposed, and enforce prompt guarding to block malicious, harmful, or non-compliant prompts before they reach the model.

The result is a safer, more reliable way to operationalize AI at scale—with guardrails built in from day one.

By capturing every tool invocation, LLM call, and decision made along the way, the AI Gateway enables full traceability across your agentic workflows—giving you true explainability and chain-of-thought visibility.

This means you can follow the exact path an agent took to reach a conclusion, from the initial user prompt through each tool it used and every response generated. Whether for debugging, auditing, or compliance, this transparent view into your AI systems helps you build trust, understand behavior, and continuously improve outcomes.

The Agent Catalog is your centralized hub for discovering, managing, and scaling autonomous agents across your organization. Much like a developer portal for APIs, it provides a unified interface where teams can explore available agents, understand their capabilities, and integrate them into workflows with ease.

Whether you’re deploying customer service bots, internal copilots, or complex orchestration agents, the Agent Catalog makes discovery intuitive and actionable.

 

Governance in the Agent Catalog goes beyond simple access control—it provides deep visibility and oversight into the full lifecycle of your agents. You can track agent lineage to understand how each agent evolved over time, from its original configuration to every update or version deployed.

Built-in agent scoring lets you measure performance, reliability, and usefulness based on real usage data, enabling continuous quality improvement and deprecation of underperforming agents. This level of governance ensures your AI ecosystem remains transparent, accountable, and high-performing at scale.

Because the AI Gateway sits in the flow of all LLM and tool traffic, you gain complete visibility into every token used, every API call made, and every agent action taken—enabling precise cost attribution and control.

This centralized traffic management lets you monitor spend in real time, set usage limits by team or application, and optimize routing to more cost-efficient models or endpoints. With this level of control, you can confidently scale AI adoption without unpredictable bills or runaway usage.
